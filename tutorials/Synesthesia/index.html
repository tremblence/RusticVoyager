☯ Synesthesia ☯






Synesthesia is the mixing of senses. Some people experience this with colors, sounds or even flavors and numbers. For example there is a guy named Daniel Tammet who sees all numbers as colors and shapes with texture; he can recite 20,000 digits of the number pi from memory and claims he sees the number as a vivid landscape.
Over time I realized I have a form of Synesthesia where all numbers and letters have a color associated. I see the number 4 as green, 5 as yellow, 6 orange etc.
The letter "a" is green, "b" is red and so on
It helps me remember words, names and numbers and I have always wanted to make an app that illustrates what I see.
