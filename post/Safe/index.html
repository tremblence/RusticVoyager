☯ Travel Safe ☯




▂   ▃   ▄   ▅   ▄   ▃   ▂         



Every country has safe places and dangerous places. You have to be careful everywhere, especially at night. A good way to be safe is to be aware, be informed and have a plan. Have a travel buddy maybe form a group.

Most people recommend you stick to the touristy spots, which I agree is safer, but many of us prefer to get off the beaten path a bit. Definitely research cities or at least talk with people about new places before you go.

▂   ▃   ▄   ▅   ▄   ▃   ▂         


Sadly some people have really nasty intentions and you have to watch out for that. I personally have been held at gunpoint by cocaine cartel in Costa Rica and escaped an attempted kidnapping/robbery in California Sounds crazy I know, but shit can happen. I definitely travel very adventurously, however in each of those cases, I ignored warnings and made a stupid decision. If you get a vibe that something is wrong or sketch, Seek Safety, do not hesitate.

I suggest you travel with a phone, and keep track of your location on Google Maps. Having a sense of where you are on a large and small scale is very important. When walking through cities or public areas, scan your surroundings and keep an eye out for suspicious looking people.

▂   ▃   ▄   ▅   ▄   ▃   ▂         


Ideally you want to be surrounded by multiple friends who you trust and have similar safety precautions in mind. If you are traveling solo, you have to be extra aware. I've never been pickpocketed but I've met dozens who have. If you can, keep all valuables in a ZIPPER pocket, if not in a secure backpack. If you want to be extra safe, carry a knife or pepper spray and learn Krav Maga.

If you have any valuables in a backpack. Keep an eye on that backpack well, best if you bring it with you everywhere. If you at at a hostel or a friends house, you can leave stuff like cloths and things people don’t normally steal. I went 6 years without having anything valuabe stolen, and I thought I had it all figured out. Then just recently someone took my backpack from under me while I was in a hammock. Shit happens- so consider bring extra-cautious. Its worth it.
