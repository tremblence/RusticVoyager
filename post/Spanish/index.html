∡  Useful Spanish Phrases ∡


Spanish, French, Italian and the other Romance Languages evolved from Vulgar Latin when the Roman Empire fell around 1000 AD. Now its the 2nd most spoken language on Earth.

Some words are Arabic in origin like guitarra and café. Nouns have either masculine or feminine ending and syllables flow together like water. It is a beautiful language spoken by beautiful people.

Here are some useful phrases and their translations.

The Spanish alphabet includes the ñ like in Año (Pronounced "on-yo")
As well as the rolled r like "por" (porrrrrr)

Noun articals



El libro        The book
Los libros        The books

La bolsa        The bag
Las bolsas        The bags


The adjective always follows the noun

el libro rojo        the red book
la bolsa roja        the red bag
las bolsas rojas        the red bags


Verb Conjugations

All verbs end with ar, er or ir

Dar        to be
Doy        I give
Das        You give
Da        He/She gives
Damos        We give
Dan          They give

Comer        to eat
como        I eat
comes        You eat
come        He/She eats
comemos        We eat
comen          They eat


You can omit the Pronoun in most cases

Como estás?       How are you?
Vamos!        Lets go! (we go)

Examples



Buenos. Soy Justin. Tengo viente cinco años.
Good (Day). I am Justin. I have twenty five years.

Me gusta surfear y ir al mercado.
I like to surf and go to the market.

Ya tengo hambre. Quiero tacos con salsa picante.
Now I have hunger. I want tacos with salsa spicy.

¿Cuanto es? Vamos a la playa.
How much is it? Lets go to the beach.

Translated very literally

Suffixes


ito / ita = Smaller

Taquito ------- little taco
Burrito ------- little donkey
Poquito ---- little little-bit
Mosquito ------- little fly
Manzanita ---- little apple
Casita -------- little house

Words ending with "ero" often refer to People

Taquero ---- taco guy
Banquero ---- banker
Empleo ----- employee
Manejero ----- driver
Viajero ----- traveler
Pescadero ----- fisherman

Words ending with "ería" often refer to Places

Taquería --- taco store
Zapatería --- shoe store
Heladería --- ice cream store
Carnicería --- meat store
Relojería --- clock store
Lavendería --- Laundry Store
