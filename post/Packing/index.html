∡ Packing List ∡



Weight adds up. Plain and simple. It can be fun to reduce weight and your back will thank you later. Recently I packed a school-sized backpack for a 3 week surf trip in Costa Rica. I knew I would be surfing most the time, so why bring too much?

▁   ▂   ▃   ▄   ▅   ▇   ▅   ▄   ▃   ▂   ▁        


I packed 2 shorts, 2 tanktops and a pair of flipflops - it ended up being more than enough. When I got to the beach I took off my shirt and didn’t put one back on till I flew back a month later.


▁   ▂   ▃   ▄   ▅   ▇   ▅   ▄   ▃   ▂   ▁        


Of course if you are headed to New York in December, you might want more than a tanktop. I learned the hard way. Pack smart but know - small backpacks make day trips easy. **And you will have more room for souvenirs!**


Here is my guide for Packing SUPER Light.    

⎈    3 shirts

⎈    2-3 pants/shorts

⎈    Shoes & Socks

⎈    Flip Flops

⎈    Tapestry/Sheet

⎈    Combination Lock

⎈    Ziplock bags

⎈    Cork Screw (wine)

⎈    Pocket knife


⎈    Phone & Charger

⎈    Portable Charger (Power Bank)

⎈    Toothbrush / Floss / Paste

⎈    Deoderant

⎈    Pen and paper

⎈    Ear Plugs

⎈    small headphones

⎈    Duct tape

⎈    Matchbooks
